YOUR PARTY PHOTOS
=================
These 8 photos fill the frames on the landing page:

  top-left.jpeg       -> silver wavy frame, upper left
  top-center.jpeg     -> blue frame w/ tape, top middle
  top-right.jpeg      -> polaroid w/ purple tape, upper right
  left-middle.jpeg    -> tall blue frame, left (Machu Picchu)
  left-lower.jpeg     -> polaroid, lower left
  right-middle.jpeg   -> silver wavy frame, right
  bottom-center.jpeg  -> blue frame w/ tape, bottom middle
  bottom-right.jpeg   -> silver frame w/ tape, bottom right

To swap any photo: replace the file here with the same name,
OR change the `src` in the photoSlots array in
src/pages/LandingPage.jsx.
